## Page 2

## From assessment to quarterly plan in four steps

*[Layout: horizontal flow with 4 numbered blocks, equal width]*

**1. Diagnose**
75–85 questions across 8 domains. Importance vs competency. Reveals the revenue leaks and operational friction your team can't see from inside.

**2. Prioritize**
Every practice ranked by P&L impact × speed × dependency × risk × company stage. Where the next leadership hour earns the highest return.

**3. Execute**
A focus portfolio picks 5–9 practices for the quarter under WIP limits matched to your lifecycle stage. Prevents scattered effort.

**4. Verify**
Every maturity upgrade requires evidence, AI-graded, senior-approved. Credible enough to bring to the board.

## Three questions to ask your team — beyond the assessment

*[Layout: numbered list, one line per item, bold lead, italic explanatory text]*

**1. Pricing power.** When was the last time we raised prices, and what happened? *Reveals revenue leak vs commercial confidence.*

**2. Unit-economics literacy.** Without looking it up, what's our blended CAC and customer LTV? *Reveals whether the team thinks like operators or like an inbox.*

**3. WIP discipline.** How many strategic initiatives are in flight right now? *More than nine = scattered effort = quiet money loss.*
